The Reality

Every day, 260 million children wake up without access to education. In developing countries, 1 in 3 children never learn to read or write.

Girls are particularly affected - 130 million girls are out of school globally. Without education, they face early marriage, limited job opportunities, and continued poverty.

The COVID-19 pandemic pushed an additional 24 million children out of school, creating the largest education disruption in history.

Your donation doesn’t just buy supplies — it gives a child a fair chance at learning, growing, and shaping their future.

Did you know? For every year a girl stays in school, her future income increases by 10-20%.

The Reality

Every night, 735 million people go to bed hungry. That's more than 1 in 10 people on our planet who don't have enough food to lead a healthy, active life.

148 million children under 5 are stunted due to chronic malnutrition. This affects their physical and mental development for life.

In developing countries, 1 in 3 children suffers from malnutrition. Every 10 seconds, a child dies from hunger-related causes.

Your kindness today helps a family eat tomorrow. Together, we can fight hunger and bring hope to every table.

Emergency Update: Climate change and conflict are pushing millions more into hunger each year.

The Reality

Every year, 5 million people die from treatable diseases simply because they can't access basic healthcare. That's like losing the entire population of Singapore every year.

2 billion people lack access to essential medicines. In many countries, basic antibiotics that cost ₹2 can save a child's life, but families can't afford them.

In developing countries, 1 in 3 people must choose between healthcare and food. Every 2 seconds, someone dies from a preventable disease.

Access to healthcare is a basic right — your small contribution can save lives and bring health security to those who need it most.

Critical Fact: Every 2 seconds, someone dies from a preventable disease due to lack of access to affordable healthcare — a crisis that can be solved with timely help and community support

The Reality

Every year, 100 million people are affected by natural disasters. From floods and earthquakes to wildfires and hurricanes, families lose everything in minutes.

In 2023 alone, 24,000 people died from natural disasters. Climate change is making these events more frequent and severe, affecting vulnerable communities the most.

When disasters strike, families lose food, shelter, medicine, and clean water. Children are especially vulnerable - they need immediate protection and care.

Your contribution enables Kind-Kart to provide timely emergency relief and essential supplies to affected communities during disasters.

Emergency Response: We aim to deploy verified aid and resources within 24 hours of any disaster event through trusted local partners.
